Use when the user wants to save, inspect, or reuse personal profile information such as email, name, location, interests, preferences, or goals.
Operations:
1) `list`: show all stored persona facts
2) `get`: fetch facts for a specific key
3) `set` or `save`: explicitly store a fact
4) `delete`: remove one fact or all values for a key
Background extraction may also infer persona facts from recent conversation history and store them automatically.
